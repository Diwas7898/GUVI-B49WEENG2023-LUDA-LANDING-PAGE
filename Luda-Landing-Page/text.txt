Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is
escpcially recognized as a Bidrohi Kobi (rebel poet) around the
country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philosopher of
Bangladesh. The famour poet Nazrul's writing deal with the
revolution of that period of the British government. His
revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of
Bangladesh. He has raised his voice again any kinda of fascism and
oppresion and got populairty swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.





Nazrul's writing explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity,
and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalish,
including religious, case-based and gender-based. Throughout his
career, Nazrul wrote short stories, novel, and eassys but is best
known for hi songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as
Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000
songs (many recored on HMV and gramophone records), collectively
known as Nazrul Geeti
